Research

My general research interests are centered around system theory, control, and optimization in complex, cyber-physical, and network systems primarily with applications to power systems.

Topics of recent interest include

  • data-driven control,

  • control in low-inertia power systems,

  • social network dynamics and formation, and

  • feedback control via online optimization.

An updated list of publications, preprints, and technical reports can be found here or on Google Scholar.
